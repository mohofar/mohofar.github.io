About Me

I am a PhD researcher in a joint program between TU Delft and ErasmusMC, focusing on the development of a 5D growth model to study hip joint growth trajectory using generative and fundamental models. My research interests lie at the intersection of artificial intelligence, medical imaging, and biomedical engineering.

Current Research

5D Growth Model for Hip Joint Development

Developing advanced computational models to understand and predict hip joint growth trajectories using a combination of generative models and fundamental biomechanical principles. This research aims to improve early diagnosis and treatment planning for developmental hip disorders.
